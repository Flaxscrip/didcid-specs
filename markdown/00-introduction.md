# Introduction

Archon is a decentralized identity and trust layer designed to enable sovereign agency for humans and AI agents. It moves identity away from centralized silos and into a content-addressed, verifiable framework.

[[def: Sovereign Identity]]:
~ An identity that is owned and controlled by the entity it represents, independent of any central authority or service provider.

[[def: Agentic Agency]]:
~ The capacity of an AI agent to act autonomously on behalf of a human, backed by verifiable credentials and secure identity.

::: note
Archon is not just a wallet; it is the infrastructure for a decentralized economy where trust is programmatic.
:::

## Goals
- Eliminate reliance on centralized identity providers.
- Provide a standard for AI agents to prove their authority.
- Enable seamless, cross-platform verifiable credential exchange.
